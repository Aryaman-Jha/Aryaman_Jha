About Me

I'm an undergraduate student in Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur. I'm also exploring the field of Cognitive Science and is registered in a minor program in that department. My interest lie in Signal Processing, Human Cognition and Computer Vision, with a dream of building an artificial agent which can mimic humans!!

Apart from academic interests, I love to do pencil sketching, kirigami, wasting my time watching animes and (Spending time alone absolutely thinking nothing... )

  • Cognitive Science
  • Computer Vision
  • Cognitive Neuroscience
  • Psychology

Education

    Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur

  • Minor in Cognitive Science, 2020
    Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur

Projects



Role of Emotional Valence on Sense of Agency

Studying the influence of emotional valence of an action outcome on sense of agency by designing an experimental setup.

Real Time Human Facial Emotion Recognition

Extracts human faces (using OpenCV haar-cascade/ dnn based classifier) from a camera stream and classifies them into 7 different moods i.e. Angry, Disgust, Fear, Happy, Sad, Surprise and Neutral.

How Close are Artificial Neural Networks to the Brain?

Study of some existing Artificial Neural Networks models to compare their structure and performance and also to realise their biological resemblance to the processing in the brain. To bridge the gap between Deep Learning and Neuroscience.

Cooperative Localization Using Posterior Linearization Belief Propagation

Implementation of a research paper, which presents the Posterior Linearization Belief Propagation algorithm for cooperative localization in Sensor Networks

Achieving CRLB in Sensor Network Estimation

Implementation of a research paper, which proposes a general framework to achieve CRLB bounds in Sensor Network Estimation

PixhawkArduinoMAVLink

Open Source Arduino library to communicate between Pixhawk and Arduino.

Optical Flow for Localisation of UAVs in Deep Tunnel

Worked on the Optical Flow algorithm based on SAD block matching to determine UAV position in deep tunnels i.e. GPS denied environment.

Real Time Human Facial Emotion Recognition

Extracts human faces (using OpenCV haar-cascade/ dnn based classifier) from a camera stream and classifies them into 7 different moods i.e. Angry, Disgust, Fear, Happy, Sad, Surprise and Neutral.

Humanoid IITK

Design and development of Institute’s first Humanoid Robot (AUTOMI). Important contributions are in the development of bipedal walking algorithm and in computer vision team.
